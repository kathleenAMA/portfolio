The Need
The need was for an interface design and interactive prototype for a medical app that allows parents to double check the dosages of the medicine they are giving to their children. For an app like this the ease of use and ability to navigate quickly was extremely important. Also any visuals used had to be accurate to ensure the parents were getting the correct information.
The Solution
The solution was to keep the steps to a minimum without overlapping any important information that might cause confusion. Using a muted colour palette that is distinguishable to those with colour vision problems allows for the colours used to display the medication to be interpreted correctly. The main focus of the visuals was to ensure that what the parents have in front of them is what they see on the app. By adding in a favourites screen it allowed parents to quickly access medication that they use on a regular basis thus saving time.
