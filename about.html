Hello there, my name is Kathleen Robertson

and I am a Web and User Experience Designer.

Web design has swept me off my feet. It was love at first site (pun intended, ah yeah), and ever since I have been working to create user friendly interfaces and provoking designs for the web.

I find learning new things exciting and exhilarating. I don't use a lack of knowledge as an excuse for not getting something done, instead I use it as the impetus to find the answer. I am driven to answer questions and resolve problems. This is why I consider Google my best friend.

I specialize in Web & UX/UI Design; I am extremely passionate about multimedia and creating interactive material.

When I'm not fighting the design crime world, by keeping comic sans off websites and conquering frustrating user experiences, I am treasure hunting through thrift stores.

Design

Design is my homegirl. I love designing, in particular for the web, but I can also throw together designs for print. If you need a snazzy new UI created, then I'm the gal for you!

Code

I love coding because it lets me develop designs in a different kind of way than pen and paper. It allows me to make interactive and creative prototypes for a vast amount of projects.

Multimedia

I am able to apply the skills I have in both design and coding together to make a unique multimedia skill set. Anywhere from promotional videos to photography - I love all types of multimedia.
